考點：密碼攻擊與實體安全

Exam: Crypto Attacks & Physical
01 — 考前重點濃縮

本封考密碼分析攻擊(旁通道/生日)與實體安全(滅火系統、冷熱通道)。兩個 D3 收尾考點。

旁通道:測時間/功耗/電磁推金鑰(打實作);生日攻擊:攻擊雜湊碰撞。
機房滅火:用潔淨氣體(FM-200/Inergen),非水;CO₂ 對人危險須先撤離;哈龍已禁。
冷熱通道:機房氣流管理,提升散熱;火災 C 級＝電氣。
02 — 核心速記
主題重點
旁通道測時間/功耗破金鑰(打實作)
生日攻擊攻擊雜湊碰撞
機房滅火潔淨氣體(非水);CO₂ 對人危險
冷熱通道氣流管理散熱
03 — 關鍵名詞（中英）
Side-channel Attack（旁通道攻擊）— 由時間/功耗推金鑰(打實作)。
Birthday Attack（生日攻擊）— 利用碰撞機率攻擊雜湊。
Clean Agent（潔淨滅火氣體）— FM-200/Inergen,機房不水損。
Hot/Cold Aisle（冷熱通道）— 機房氣流管理。
Class C Fire（C 級火災）— 電氣火災。
04 — 常見陷阱

旁通道打『實作』非『數學』;對策常數時間實作。

機房不用水滅火(毀設備);CO₂ 對人有害需先撤離;哈龍已禁。

生日攻擊針對雜湊碰撞,所需嘗試遠少於暴力。

05 — 🎯 實戰考題
QUESTION 1

An attacker measures the power consumption and timing of a smart card's cryptographic operations to recover the key. This is a:

（攻擊者量測智慧卡密碼運算的功耗與時間以還原金鑰。這是:）

A. Brute-force attack 暴力攻擊
B. Side-channel attack 旁通道攻擊
C. Birthday attack 生日攻擊
D. Dictionary attack 字典攻擊
✅ 解答與解析

正解：B。由實體量測(功耗/時間/電磁)推測金鑰＝旁通道攻擊;它打『實作』而非演算法數學。

為何其他選項錯：
  • A：暴力是窮舉金鑰,非量測物理特徵。
  • C：生日攻擊針對雜湊碰撞。
  • D：字典攻擊試常見密碼。
QUESTION 2

Which fire suppression approach is MOST appropriate for a data center to avoid damaging equipment?

（為避免損壞設備,資料中心最適合的滅火方式為何?）

A. Wet pipe sprinklers 濕式撒水
B. Clean agent gas (e.g., FM-200) 潔淨氣體
C. Water deluge 大量灑水
D. Foam 泡沫
✅ 解答與解析

正解：B。機房應用潔淨氣體(FM-200/Inergen)滅火,不損電子設備且不水損;水/泡沫會毀設備。

為何其他選項錯：
  • A／C：水會損壞電子設備、造成短路。
  • D：泡沫殘留會損壞設備,不適合機房。
QUESTION 3

A birthday attack is MOST directly a threat to which cryptographic component?

（生日攻擊最直接威脅哪個密碼學元件?）

A. Symmetric key length 對稱金鑰長度
B. Hash function collision resistance 雜湊函數抗碰撞性
C. Random number generation 亂數產生
D. Certificate validity period 憑證有效期
✅ 解答與解析

正解：B。生日攻擊利用機率尋找雜湊碰撞,直接威脅雜湊的抗碰撞性;故需足夠長的雜湊(SHA-256+)。

為何其他選項錯：
  • A：金鑰長度主要對抗暴力攻擊。
  • C：亂數品質影響金鑰可預測性,非生日攻擊核心。
  • D：與憑證有效期無關。
06 — (ISC)² 選最佳答案思路

攻擊辨識:量測物理特徵→旁通道;雜湊碰撞→生日;窮舉→暴力;常見值→字典。

機房滅火一律選『潔淨氣體(非水)』;CO₂ 對人危險;哈龍已禁。

✏️ 一句話總結 + 自我檢測（點開看解答）

一句話：旁通道打實作(功耗/時間)、生日攻擊打雜湊碰撞;機房用潔淨氣體滅火(非水);冷熱通道散熱。

Q1 . 量測功耗/時間還原金鑰是什麼攻擊？
→ 旁通道攻擊(side-channel,打實作)。

Q2 . 資料中心最適合的滅火方式？
→ 潔淨氣體(FM-200/Inergen),非水。

Q3 . 生日攻擊威脅什麼？
→ 雜湊函數的抗碰撞性。

🎯 實戰演練 EXTRA
QUESTION 4

During a cryptanalysis engagement, an attacker is able to submit arbitrary plaintexts of her own choosing to the encryption system and collect the resulting ciphertexts to deduce information about the key. Which attack model is this?

（密碼分析演練中,攻擊者可『自行選定』任意明文送入加密系統,並收集對應密文以推導金鑰資訊。這是哪種攻擊模型?）

A. Known-plaintext attack 已知明文攻擊
B. Chosen-plaintext attack 選擇明文攻擊
C. Ciphertext-only attack 唯密文攻擊
D. Pass-the-hash attack 傳遞雜湊攻擊
✅ 解答與解析

正解：B。關鍵字『可自行選定(chosen)任意明文並取得對應密文』→ 選擇明文攻擊(CPA);它比已知明文更強(攻擊者能主動控制輸入),差分密碼分析即屬此類。記憶:能『選』=chosen,只是『有』=known。

為何其他選項錯：
  • A：已知明文是攻擊者『手上剛好有』明密文對,無法自選明文——與 chosen 只差一字,是本題最強干擾項。
  • C：唯密文攻擊只有密文可用,是最弱的攻擊模型。
  • D：Pass-the-Hash 是竊取密碼雜湊直接用於認證,屬憑證攻擊而非密碼分析模型。
QUESTION 5

A defense facility processing classified data is concerned that attackers in a nearby building could reconstruct screen contents from electromagnetic emanations. Which countermeasure BEST addresses this risk?

（某處理機密資料的國防設施擔心鄰棟攻擊者可由電磁洩漏重建螢幕內容。哪個對策最能處理此風險?）

A. Full-disk encryption 全磁碟加密
B. TEMPEST-rated shielding (e.g., Faraday cage) TEMPEST 等級屏蔽(如法拉第籠)
C. Upgrading to AES-256 encryption 升級至 AES-256 加密
D. Network intrusion detection system 網路入侵偵測系統
✅ 解答與解析

正解：B。電磁洩漏(emanation)攻擊擷取的是顯示/處理中的明文訊號,加密幫不上忙;對策是 TEMPEST 等級設備、屏蔽線材/法拉第籠、白噪音與管制區(control zone)。判斷邏輯:威脅在『物理訊號外洩』,就要用物理屏蔽對付。

為何其他選項錯：
  • A：磁碟加密保護『靜態儲存』;螢幕顯示時資料已解密,電磁訊號照樣洩漏——聽起來對但保護錯層,是本題最強干擾項。
  • C：加密演算法強度與空中電磁洩漏無關。
  • D：NIDS 監看網路流量,偵測不到不經網路的電磁側漏。
QUESTION 6

A fire alarm triggers in a data center protected by a CO2 flooding suppression system. What is the FIRST priority before the agent discharges?

（資料中心的 CO₂ 全區滅火系統因火警觸發。在氣體釋放前的『第一』優先事項是?）

A. Gracefully shut down critical servers 優雅關閉關鍵伺服器
B. Ensure all personnel have evacuated the room 確認所有人員已撤離該區
C. Notify senior management 通報高階主管
D. Fail over to the disaster recovery site 切換至災難復原站點
✅ 解答與解析

正解：B。CO₂ 靠排除氧氣滅火,對人員致命——因此釋放前必須先確認人員撤離。判斷邏輯:CISSP 鐵律『人命安全永遠優先於資產與營運』,任何實體安全情境題先想 people first。

為何其他選項錯：
  • A：保護設備排在保護人命之後——技術上合理但優先序錯,是本題最強干擾項。
  • C：通報屬後續溝通程序,非釋放前第一優先。
  • D：營運持續(切換 DR)是火災處理後段的動作。

▍攻擊辨識

Pass-the-Hash＝用擷取的雜湊直接認證（不需破解）；MITM＝中間人各建一條會談；Side-channel＝功耗/時間/電磁推敲金鑰（TEMPEST）。Meet-in-the-middle→2DES。

▍實體考點

火災選滅火劑（電器→C/CO2）、UPS vs 發電機角色；任何情境人命安全優先於資產。

對應官方 §3.7 攻擊、§3.9.8 防火（考點）。

§3.7.11 Pass the hash
• Pass the hash (PtH): a technique where an attacker captures a password hash (as opposed to the password characters) and then simply passes it through for authentication and potentially lateral access to other networked systems
• the threat actor doesn’t need to decrypt the hash to obtain a plain text password
• PtH attacks exploit the authentication protocol, as the password's hash remains static for every session until the password is rotated
• attackers commonly obtain hashes by scraping a system's active memory and other techniques
• PtH attacks typically exploit NTLM vulns, but attackers also use similar attacks against other protocols, including Kerberos
• Countermeasures: defense-in-depth strategy that focuses on preventing initial access, securing credentials, and restricting lateral movement
§3.7.7 Side-channel
• Side-channel: these attacks seek to use the way computer systems generate characteristic footprints of activity, such as changes in processor utilization, power consumption, or electromagnetic radiation to monitor system activity and retrieve information that is actively being encrypted
• similar to an implementation attack, side-channel attacks look for weaknesses outside of the core cryptography functions themselves
• a side-channel attack could target a computer’s CPU, or attempt to gain key information about the environment during encryption or decryption by looking for electromagnetic emissions or the amount of execution time required during decryption
• side-channel characteristics information are often combined together to try to break down the cryptography
• timing attack is an example
• the US government and NATO have long been aware of this vulnerability (referring to the associated security standards as TEMPEST)
• Countermeasures:
• physical device/cable shielding
• use TEMPEST-rated equipment
• introduce random noise into signals (signal obfuscation)
• maintain physical security
§3.9.8 Fire prevention, detection, and suppression
• Protecting personnel from harm should always be the most important goal of any security or protection system!
• In addition to protecting people, fire detection and suppression is designed to keep asset damage caused by fire, smoke, heat, and suppression materials to a minimum
• Fire triangle: three triangle corners represent fuel, heat, and oxygen; the center of the triangle represents the chemical reaction among these three elements
• if you can remove any one of the three items from the fire triangle, the fire can be extinguished
• Fire suppression mediums:
• water suppresses temperature
• soda acid and other dry powders suppress the fuel supply
• carbon dioxide (CO2) suppresses the oxygen supply
• halon substitutes and other nonflammable gases interfere with the chemistry of combustion and/or suppress the oxygen supply
• Fire stages:
• Stage 1: incipient stage: at this stage, there is only air ionization and no smoke
• Stage 2: smoke stage: smoke is visible from the point of ignition
• Stage 3: flame stage: this is when a flame can be seen with the naked eye
• Stage 4: heat stage: at stage 4, there is an intense heat buildup and everything in the area burns
• Fire extinguisher classes:
• Class A: common combustibles
• Class B: liquids
• Class C: electrical
• Class D: metal
• Class K: cooking material (oil/grease)
• Four main types of suppression:
• wet pipe system: (AKA closed head system): is always filled with water; water discharges immediately when suppression is triggered
• dry pipe system: contains compressed inert gas
• pre-action system: a variation of the dry pipe system that uses a two-stage detection and release mechanism
• deluge system: uses larger pipes and delivers larger volume of water
• Note: Most sprinkler heads feature a glass bulb filled with a glycerin-based liquid; this liquid expands when it comes in contact with air heated to between 135 and 165 degrees; when the liquid expands, it shatters its glass confines and the sprinkler head activates
