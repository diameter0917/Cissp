考點：稽核獨立性與度量

Exam: Audit Independence & Metrics
01 — 考前重點濃縮

本封考稽核獨立性與 KPI vs KRI、管理審查時機。

稽核者不得查核自己的工作;內部稽核向董事會/審計委員會直報以保獨立。
KPI 績效(回顧)、KRI 風險(預警)。
對外公信力→第三方稽核;內部改善→內部稽核/評估。
02 — 核心速記
概念重點
稽核獨立性不查自己的工作、向董事會直報
KPI績效(回顧)
KRI風險預警(前瞻)
第三方稽核對外公信力
03 — 關鍵名詞（中英）
Auditor Independence（稽核獨立性）— 不查核自己的工作。
KPI（關鍵績效指標）— 衡量績效(回顧)。
KRI（關鍵風險指標）— 前瞻風險預警。
Management Review（管理審查）— 定期檢視與決策。
Third-party Audit（第三方稽核）— 獨立、對外公信。
04 — 常見陷阱

稽核者不可查自己的工作(獨立性=職責分離延伸)。

KPI(績效) vs KRI(風險預警)別反。

對外公信用第三方;內部稽核應向治理層直報。

05 — 🎯 實戰考題
QUESTION 1

Why should an internal auditor NOT audit a process they personally designed and operate?

（為何內部稽核員不應稽核自己親自設計並操作的流程?）

A. It takes too long 太花時間
B. It violates auditor independence/objectivity 違反稽核獨立性/客觀性
C. It requires special tools 需要特殊工具
D. It is more expensive 較昂貴
✅ 解答與解析

正解：B。稽核者查核自己負責的工作會喪失獨立性與客觀性(球員兼裁判),屬職責分離的延伸。

為何其他選項錯：
  • A／C／D：非核心原因;關鍵是獨立性受損。
QUESTION 2

Which metric is FORWARD-looking and designed to provide an early warning that risk is increasing?

（哪種指標是『前瞻性』的,旨在提供風險上升的早期預警?）

A. KPI 關鍵績效指標
B. KRI 關鍵風險指標
C. ROI 投資報酬率
D. SLA 服務水準協議
✅ 解答與解析

正解：B。KRI(關鍵風險指標)是前瞻性的風險預警(如未修補高風險弱點數趨勢);KPI 衡量過去/現在績效。

為何其他選項錯：
  • A：KPI 是回顧型績效衡量。
  • C：ROI 是財務報酬。
  • D：SLA 是服務水準約定。
QUESTION 3

An organization wants an assessment of its security controls that will carry credibility with external customers. Who should perform it?

（某組織想要一份對外部客戶具公信力的安全控制評估。應由誰執行?）

A. The internal IT team 內部 IT 團隊
B. The system owner 系統擁有者
C. An independent third-party auditor 獨立第三方稽核員
D. The development team 開發團隊
✅ 解答與解析

正解：C。要對外具公信力,應由獨立第三方稽核員執行(獨立性提供客觀保證)。

為何其他選項錯：
  • A／B／D：內部人員缺乏對外公信力的獨立性。
06 — (ISC)² 選最佳答案思路

稽核獨立性=不查自己的工作、向治理層直報;對外公信→第三方。

KRI 前瞻預警、KPI 回顧績效。

✏️ 一句話總結 + 自我檢測（點開看解答）

一句話：稽核獨立性(不查自己的工作);KPI 績效/KRI 風險預警;對外公信用第三方稽核。

Q1 . 為何不能稽核自己設計的流程？
→ 違反稽核獨立性/客觀性(球員兼裁判)。

Q2 . 前瞻性風險預警指標是？
→ KRI(關鍵風險指標)。

Q3 . 對外具公信力的評估該由誰做？
→ 獨立第三方稽核員。

對應官方 §6.5 獨立性、§6.3.3 度量（考點）。

§6.5.1 Internal
• Having an internal team conduct security audits has several advantages:
• understanding the internal environment reduces time
• an internal team can delve into all parts of systems, because they have insider knowledge
• internal auditors can be more agile in adapting to changing needs, rescheduling failed assessment components quickly
• Disadvantages of using an internal team to conduct security audits:
• the team may have limited exposure to new/other methodologies (e.g. the team may have depth but not breadth of experience and knowledge)
• potential conflicts of interest (e.g. reluctance to throw other teams under the bus and accurately report their findings)
• audit team members may start with an agenda (say to secure funding) and overstate faults, or have interpersonal motives
§6.3.3 Key performance and risk indicators
• Key Performance Indicators (KPIs): measures that provide significance of showing the performance of an ISMS compared to stated goals; KPIs are backward looking
• Choose the factors that can show the state of security
• Define baselines for some (or better yet all) of the factors
• Develop a plan for periodically capturing factor values (use automation!)
• Analyze and interpret the data and report the results
• Key metrics or KPIs that should be monitored by security managers may vary from org to org, but could include:
• number of open vulns
• time to resolve vulns
• vulnerability/defect recurrence
• number of compromised accounts
• number of software flaws detected in pre-production scanning
• repeat audit findings
• user attempts to visit known malicious sites
• Develop a dashboard of metrics and track them
• Key Risk Indicators (KRIs): indicate the level of exposure to operational risk; they help monitor potential future shifts in risk conditions or emerging risks; KRIs are forward-looking; example KRIs might include:
• percentage of systems running unsupported/EOL software
• number of third-party vendors without current security assessments
• volume of unencrypted sensitive data transmissions
