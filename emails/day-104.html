考點：生物特徵與身分生命週期

Exam: Biometrics & Lifecycle
01 — 考前重點濃縮

本封考生物特徵指標(FAR/FRR/CER)與身分生命週期(deprovisioning 時機)。

FAR(誤接受,安全風險)、FRR(誤拒絕,可用性)、CER(交叉點,越低越準)。
離職應即時 deprovisioning;轉調權限隨之調整(防 privilege creep)。
調靈敏度:降 FAR 會升 FRR(反之亦然)。
02 — 指標速記
指標意義
FAR Type II誤接受冒充者(安全風險)
FRR Type I誤拒絕本人(可用性)
CERFAR=FRR 之點,越低越準
03 — 關鍵名詞（中英）
FAR（誤接受率）— 把冒充者當本人(安全風險)。
FRR（誤拒絕率）— 把本人當冒充者(可用性)。
CER（交叉錯誤率）— FAR=FRR,越低越準。
Deprovisioning（撤銷佈建）— 離職即時停用帳號。
Privilege Creep（權限蔓延）— 轉調未回收致累積。
04 — 常見陷阱

CER 越低越好(系統越準);別誤以為越高越好。

FAR=安全、FRR=可用;調一個會影響另一個。

離職撤銷要即時(批次易留殭屍帳號)。

05 — 🎯 實戰考題
QUESTION 1

When comparing two biometric systems, which metric BEST indicates overall accuracy, where a LOWER value is better?

（比較兩套生物辨識系統時,哪個指標最能代表整體準確度,且『數值越低越好』?）

A. FAR 誤接受率
B. FRR 誤拒絕率
C. CER 交叉錯誤率
D. Throughput 處理量
✅ 解答與解析

正解：C。CER(交叉錯誤率)是 FAR=FRR 的交叉點,代表整體準確度;CER 越低,系統越準。

為何其他選項錯：
  • A：FAR 只看誤接受一面。
  • B：FRR 只看誤拒絕一面。
  • D：處理量是速度指標,非準確度。
QUESTION 2

Which biometric error is the MOST serious from a security standpoint because it admits an impostor?

（從安全角度看,哪種生物辨識錯誤最嚴重,因為它讓冒充者通過?）

A. False Rejection Rate (FRR) 誤拒絕率
B. False Acceptance Rate (FAR) 誤接受率
C. Crossover Error Rate (CER)
D. Enrollment time 註冊時間
✅ 解答與解析

正解：B。FAR(誤接受)讓冒充者被當成本人通過,是最嚴重的安全風險;FRR 只影響合法者的可用性。

為何其他選項錯：
  • A：FRR 是擋到本人(可用性問題),非安全風險。
  • C：CER 是綜合指標,非單一錯誤。
  • D：與註冊速度無關。
QUESTION 3

What is the MOST important access-management action to take immediately when an employee is terminated?

（員工被資遣時,最重要的存取管理動作是立即:）

A. Rotate their job duties 輪調其職務
B. Disable/deprovision their accounts 停用/撤銷其帳號
C. Conduct annual access review 進行年度存取審查
D. Increase password length 增加密碼長度
✅ 解答與解析

正解：B。離職時應即時停用/撤銷帳號(deprovisioning),防止前員工或攻擊者利用殘留帳號。

為何其他選項錯：
  • A：輪調是在職控制,與離職無關。
  • C：年度審查太慢,離職須即時處理。
  • D：與離職帳號處理無關。
06 — (ISC)² 選最佳答案思路

CER 越低越準;FAR=安全(最嚴重)、FRR=可用。

離職→即時 deprovisioning;轉調→權限隨之調整(防 privilege creep)。

✏️ 一句話總結 + 自我檢測（點開看解答）

一句話：FAR 誤接受(安全最嚴)、FRR 誤拒絕(可用)、CER 越低越準;離職即時撤銷帳號。

Q1 . 生物系統哪個指標越低越好？
→ CER(交叉錯誤率)。

Q2 . 從安全看哪種生物錯誤最嚴重？
→ FAR(誤接受,放冒充者進來)。

Q3 . 員工資遣時最重要的存取動作？
→ 立即停用/撤銷帳號(deprovisioning)。

🎯 實戰演練 EXTRA
QUESTION 4

A security manager is configuring a fingerprint reader for a restricted server room. Management states that admitting an unauthorized person is unacceptable, even at the cost of employee inconvenience. How should the system sensitivity be tuned?

（安全經理為管制機房設定指紋辨識。管理層明示:「放進未授權者」絕不可接受,即使犧牲員工便利也在所不惜。系統靈敏度應如何調校?）

A. Operate exactly at the CER for the best balance 設在 CER 交叉點取得最佳平衡
B. Decrease sensitivity to minimize false rejections 調低靈敏度,把誤拒降到最低
C. Choose the device with the highest throughput 選處理量最高的設備
D. Increase sensitivity to minimize FAR, accepting a higher FRR 調高靈敏度把 FAR 壓到最低,接受較高 FRR
✅ 解答與解析

正解：D。FAR 與 FRR 是蹺蹺板:調高靈敏度→FAR 降、FRR 升。管理層已把風險胃納說死——「絕不可誤放冒充者」,所以應犧牲便利(容忍較高 FRR)換取最低 FAR。調校方向永遠由「風險決策」決定:高安全區壓 FAR,一般便利區才考慮平衡。

為何其他選項錯：
  • A：最強干擾項——CER 是「比較設備準確度」的指標;營運調校點應依風險需求偏移,高安全區不該停在平衡點。
  • B：把便利放在安全之前,與管理層的風險決策相反(FRR 低=FAR 高)。
  • C：處理量是速度/容量指標,與防止誤接受無關。
QUESTION 5

An audit at a manufacturing firm finds dozens of service accounts whose passwords have not changed in years, several holding domain administrator privileges, with no record of which application uses which account. What should the security team do FIRST?

（某製造業稽核發現數十個服務帳號多年未改密碼,其中數個持有網域管理員權限,且無任何紀錄可知哪個應用程式使用哪個帳號。安全團隊「首先」應做什麼?）

A. Disable all service accounts until owners come forward 先停用所有服務帳號,等使用者自行認領
B. Immediately rotate every service account password 立即輪換所有服務帳號密碼
C. Inventory the accounts, assign owners, and document required privileges 先盤點帳號、指定負責人並記錄所需權限
D. Purchase a PAM platform to vault the credentials 採購 PAM 平台把憑證入庫
✅ 解答與解析

正解：C。機器/服務身分治理的第一步永遠是盤點與當責:知道有哪些帳號、誰負責、各需要多少權限,之後的降權(最小權限)、輪換、入庫 PAM 才有依據。「不知道就無法管理」——在不明用途前貿然動帳號,會弄壞生產系統。Think like a manager:先建立可視性與當責,再做技術處置。

為何其他選項錯：
  • A：不知道哪個帳號撐著哪條產線就全面停用,等於自己對工廠發動阻斷攻擊。
  • B：方向對但順序錯——不知道哪個應用綁定哪個帳號就輪換,會造成大規模服務中斷。
  • D：PAM 是好的「後續」解方,但沒有盤點清單,連要把哪些憑證入庫都不知道。
QUESTION 6

A hospital plans to authenticate staff with fingerprint templates stored in a central database. From a security standpoint, what is the MOST significant concern about a breach of this database?

（某醫院計畫以集中資料庫儲存員工指紋樣板做驗證。從安全角度看,該資料庫外洩「最重大」的隱憂是什麼?）

A. Biometric characteristics cannot be revoked or reissued like a password 生物特徵不像密碼,外洩後無法撤銷或換發
B. Templates consume excessive storage space 樣板占用過多儲存空間
C. Encrypting templates slows down each authentication 樣板加密會拖慢每次驗證
D. Staff may object to enrollment on privacy grounds 員工可能以隱私為由抗拒註冊
✅ 解答與解析

正解：A。密碼外洩可重設、Token 遺失可換發,但指紋一輩子只有一組——樣板外洩後受害者「永久」失去該因子的可信度,無法撤銷或補發。這是生物特徵(Type 3)獨有的風險,所以樣板庫必須以最高等級保護(加密、存取控制、甚至只存不可逆的特徵值)。

為何其他選項錯：
  • B：樣板通常很小,儲存成本是營運瑣事,非安全隱憂。
  • C：加密開銷極低,且保護樣板本來就是必要控制,不構成反對理由。
  • D：接受度/隱私顧慮是真實的「導入」議題,但題目問的是「外洩」的最重大後果——不可撤銷性才是安全核心。

▍生物特徵錯誤率

Type I 錯誤＝FRR 誤拒率（真人被拒，影響便利）；Type II 錯誤＝FAR 誤受率（冒充者被接受，安全風險較大）；CER/EER 交叉錯誤率＝FAR=FRR 之點，越低越準。

▍生命週期時機

離職須即時 deprovision（exit interview 前/當下停權）；換崗要重檢權限防 creep。

對應官方 §5.2.4 身分證明、§5.5 生命週期（考點）。

§5.2.4 Registration, proofing, and establishment of identity
• Within an organization, new employees prove their identity with appropriate documentation during the hiring process
• in-person identity proofing includes things like passport, DL, birth cert etc
• Online orgs often use knowledge-based authentication (KBA) for identity-proofing of someone new (e.g. a new customer creating a new bank/savings account)
• example questions include past vehicle purchases, amount of mortgage payment, previous addresses, DL numbers
• they then query authoritative information (e.g. credit bureaus or gov agencies) for matches
• Cognitive Passwords: security questions that are gathered during account creation, which are later used as questions for authentication (e.g. name of pet, color of first car etc)
• one of the flaws associated with cognitive passwords is that the information is often available on social media sites or general internet searches
§5.5.2 Provisioning and deprovisioning (e.g., on/off boarding and transfers)
• Identity and access provisioning lifecycle refers to the creation, management, and deletion of accounts
• this lifecycle is important because without properly defined and maintained user accounts, a system is unable to establish accurate identity, perform authentication, provide authorization, and track accountability
• Privileged Access Management (PAM): a critical component of the identity and access provisioning lifecycle; PAM solutions manage, monitor, and audit privileged account access and include features like session recording, credential vaulting, and just-in-time privileged access
• Provisioning/Onboarding
• provisioning ensures that accounts have appropriate privileges based on task requirements and employees receive needed hardware; said another way, includes the creation, maintenance, and removal of user objects from apps, systems, and directories
• proper user account creation, or provisioning, ensures that personnel follow specific procedures when creating accounts
• new-user account creation is AKA enrollment or registration
• automated provisioning: information is provided to an app, that then creates the accounts via pre-defined rules (assigning to appropriate groups based on roles)
• automated provisioning systems create accounts consistently
• workflow provisioning: provisioning that occurs through an established workflow, like an HR process
• provisioning also includes issuing hardware, tokens, smartcards etc to employees
• it’s important to keep accurate records when issuing hardware to employees
• after provisioning, an org can follow up with onboarding processes, including:
• the employee reads and signs the acceptable use policy (AUP)
• explaining security best practices (like infected emails)
• reviewing the mobile device policy
• ensuring the employee’s computer is operational, and they can log in
• configure a password manager
• explaining how to access help desk
• show how to access, share and save resources
• Deprovisioning/Offboarding
• deprovisioning processes disable or delete an account when employees leave, and offboarding processes ensure that employees return all hardware the org issued them
• deprovisioning/offboarding occurs when an employee leaves the organization or is transferred to a different department
• account revocation: deleting an account is the easiest way to deprovision
• an employee's account is usually first disabled
• supervisors can then review the user’s data and determine if anything is needed
• note: if terminated employee retains access to a user account after the exit interview, the risk for sabotage is very high
• deprovisioning includes collecting any hardware issued to an employee such as laptops, mobile devices and auth tokens
