考點：維運控制與特權

Exam: Ops Controls & Privilege
01 — 考前重點濃縮

本封考維運人事控制(SoD/輪調/休假)與特權管理。判斷情境用哪個防舞弊控制。

SoD 職責分離:拆關鍵任務防單人獨力作惡;強制休假/輪調:揭發藏匿舞弊。
最小權限+需知;特權用 PAM(JIT/零常駐特權)。
Dual control:同一高敏感動作需兩人。
02 — 控制速記
控制防什麼
SoD 職責分離單人獨力完成關鍵交易
強制休假持續維護的舞弊
輪調長期藏匿舞弊
Dual control單人執行高敏感動作
03 — 關鍵名詞（中英）
Separation of Duties（職責分離）— 拆關鍵任務防舞弊。
Mandatory Vacation（強制休假）— 他人接手揭弊。
Job Rotation（工作輪調）— 換崗揭弊。
Dual Control（雙人控制）— 高敏感動作需兩人。
Least Privilege（最小權限）— 只給所需。
04 — 常見陷阱

SoD(分步驟) vs Dual control(同動作兩人)。

強制休假=偵測舞弊(他人接手發現異常)。

need to know(資訊) vs least privilege(權限)。

05 — 🎯 實戰考題
QUESTION 1

Requiring that the person who creates a payment cannot also approve it is an example of:

（要求『建立付款的人不能同時核准付款』,這是哪種控制的例子?）

A. Job rotation 工作輪調
B. Separation of duties 職責分離
C. Least privilege 最小權限
D. Mandatory vacation 強制休假
✅ 解答與解析

正解：B。把『建立』與『核准』拆給不同人,使單人無法獨力完成並隱匿弊端＝職責分離(SoD)。

為何其他選項錯：
  • A：輪調是定期換崗,非拆分單一交易步驟。
  • C：最小權限是限制權限範圍,非拆分職責。
  • D：強制休假是讓他人接手以揭弊。
QUESTION 2

Which control is MOST effective at detecting fraud committed by an employee who must continuously maintain it to hide it?

（對於『員工須持續親自維護以隱藏的舞弊』,哪個控制最能偵測出來?）

A. Mandatory vacation 強制休假
B. Encryption 加密
C. Firewall 防火牆
D. Antivirus 防毒
✅ 解答與解析

正解：A。強制休假讓當事人離崗、由他人接手其工作,需持續維護的舞弊(做假帳/挪用)便會浮現,屬偵測+嚇阻控制。

為何其他選項錯：
  • B／C／D：技術控制無法偵測這類人為舞弊。
QUESTION 3

What is the difference between 'need to know' and 'least privilege'?

（『需知(need to know)』與『最小權限(least privilege)』的差別是什麼?）

A. They are identical 完全相同
B. Need to know restricts information access; least privilege restricts permissions/actions 需知限制資訊存取;最小權限限制權限/動作
C. Need to know applies only to managers 需知只適用主管
D. Least privilege only applies to data 最小權限只適用資料
✅ 解答與解析

正解：B。需知限制『可接觸的資訊』(知不知道);最小權限限制『可執行的權限/動作』(能不能做)。常並用。

為何其他選項錯：
  • A：兩者面向不同,非完全相同。
  • C／D：無此限制。
06 — (ISC)² 選最佳答案思路

拆分步驟=SoD;同動作兩人=dual control;強制休假/輪調=揭弊。

需知=資訊、最小權限=權限;特權用 PAM/JIT。

✏️ 一句話總結 + 自我檢測（點開看解答）

一句話：SoD 拆步驟防舞弊、強制休假/輪調揭弊、dual control 同動作兩人;需知(資訊) vs 最小權限(權限)。

Q1 . 『建立付款者不可核准付款』是哪種控制？
→ 職責分離(SoD)。

Q2 . 揭發須持續維護的舞弊最有效控制？
→ 強制休假(mandatory vacation)。

Q3 . 需知與最小權限的差別？
→ 需知限制資訊、最小權限限制權限/動作。

🎯 實戰演練 EXTRA
QUESTION 4

Opening the data center safe that stores recovery credentials requires two custodians to be present and act together at the same time. This control is BEST described as:

（開啟存放復原憑證的資料中心保險櫃,需要兩名保管人同時在場、共同操作。這個控制最適合稱為:）

A. Separation of duties 職責分離
B. Job rotation 工作輪調
C. Least privilege 最小權限
D. Dual control (two-person control) 雙人控制
✅ 解答與解析

正解：D。同一個高敏感『動作』需要兩人同時執行＝dual control(two-person control)。鑑別點:SoD 是把『流程拆成不同步驟』分給不同人;dual control 是『同一動作本身』就要兩人到場——本題只有一個動作(開保險櫃),所以是 dual control。

為何其他選項錯：
  • A：SoD 拆的是流程步驟(如建立 vs 核准),題幹沒有拆步驟。
  • B：輪調是定期換崗以揭弊,與同時到場無關。
  • C：最小權限限制權限範圍,不要求兩人共同操作。
QUESTION 5

An organization wants to reduce the risk posed by administrators' standing privileged accounts. Which approach is BEST?

（組織想降低管理員『常駐特權帳號』帶來的風險。哪種做法最佳?）

A. Use a PAM solution to grant just-in-time privileged access with zero standing privileges 用 PAM 以 JIT 方式授予特權,常駐特權歸零
B. Create one shared admin account protected by a very strong password 建立一個共用管理帳號並設定超強密碼
C. Keep permanent admin rights but review them once a year 保留永久管理權限,每年審查一次
D. Reassign admin privileges to managers instead of engineers 把管理權限改給主管而非工程師
✅ 解答與解析

正解：A。特權帳號管理的最佳實務是 PAM＋JIT(just-in-time):需要時才臨時取得、用完即回收,常駐特權(standing privileges)歸零,把憑證被竊後的可利用窗口縮到最小。

為何其他選項錯：
  • B：共用帳號破壞可歸責性(accountability),密碼再強也無法追到個人。
  • C：永久特權+年審,風險 365 天常駐,審查窗口過長。
  • D：換人持有不會降低風險,且違反依職務需要授權的原則。
QUESTION 6

Beyond deterring and detecting fraud, what ADDITIONAL operational benefit does job rotation provide?

（除了嚇阻與偵測舞弊,工作輪調還提供什麼『額外的』維運效益?）

A. It eliminates the need for separation of duties 從此不再需要職責分離
B. Cross-training creates personnel redundancy so no single person is indispensable 交叉訓練形成人力備援,不會有人變得不可或缺
C. It lets employees accumulate more privileges over time 讓員工隨時間累積更多權限
D. It removes the need for audit logging 從此不再需要稽核日誌
✅ 解答與解析

正解：B。輪調的第二效益是知識轉移/交叉訓練:多人會做同一職務,形成人力備援,降低『關鍵人物單點失效』(離職/休假/事故)的營運風險——這也是維運韌性的一環。

為何其他選項錯：
  • A：輪調與 SoD 互補,不能互相取代。
  • C：權限累積(privilege creep)是該被清理的問題;輪調時應重新授權,而非累積。
  • D：稽核日誌仍是基本偵測控制,與輪調無替代關係。

▍控制辨識

「限制能看哪些資訊」→Need-to-know；「限制權限多寡」→Least Privilege；「關鍵功能需多人」→SoD/two-person；「金鑰分持」→split knowledge。

Job rotation/Mandatory vacation 是偵測型控制（揭露舞弊）；PAM 監控特權帳號。

對應官方 §7.4（維運控制與特權，考點）。

§7.4.1 Need-to-know/least privilege
• Need-to-Know: principle restricts access to information or resources to only those individuals who require it to perform their specific tasks or duties
• focus: protects sensitive information by limiting what someone can access
• Least Privilege: principle that limits the access rights of users, processes, or systems to the minimum level necessary to perform their job functions; states that subjects are granted only the privileges necessary to perform assigned work tasks and no more
• focus: restricts how much access a user or system has (permissions)
• privilege in this context includes both permissions to data and rights to perform systems tasks
• limiting and controlling privileges based on this concept protects confidentiality and data integrity
• principle relies on the assumption that all users have a well-defined job description that personnel understand
• least privilege is typically focused on ensuring that user privileges are restricted, but it also applies to apps or processes (e.g. if an app or service is compromised, the attacker can assume the service account’s privileges)
• Need to know and least privilege principle are two standard IT security principles implemented in secure networks; they limit access to data and systems so users and other subjects can access only what they require; this limited access helps prevent security incidents and helps limit the scope of incidents when they occur; when not followed, security incidents result in far greater damage to an org
§7.4.3 Privileged account management
• Privileged entities are trusted, but they can abuse privileges, and it's therefore essential to monitor all assignments of privileged operations
• The goal is to ensure that trusted employees do not abuse the special privileges that are granted; monitoring these operations can also detect many attacks, because attackers commonly use special privileges during an attack
• Advanced privileged account management practices can limit the time users have advanced privileges
• Privileged Account Management (PAM): solutions that restrict access to privileged accounts or detect when accounts use any elevated privileges (e.g. admin accounts)
• Microsoft domains, this includes local admin accounts, Domain and Enterprise Admins groups
• Linux includes root or sudo accounts
• PAM solutions should monitor actions taken by privileged accounts, new user accounts, new routes to a router table, altering config of a firewall, accessing system log and audit files
